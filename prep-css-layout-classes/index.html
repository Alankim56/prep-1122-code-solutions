Ironman

Ironman 1

In Ironman 1, genius multibillionare playboy, Tony Stark, develops the first iron man suit in his repetoir. His path is obstructed by those trying to steal his technology.

Ironman 2

In Ironman 2, Tony Stark is faced with another challenge in which governments try and replicate his work regarding the iron man suits. He must find a way in which he can keep the technology to himself.

Ironman 3

In Ironman 3, Tony stark fights against a new type in weapon in which humans are genetically modified to become evolved. He faces a challenge in which his lover gets taken hostage by the main antgonist.
